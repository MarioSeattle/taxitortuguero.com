Let us show you the beauties of Tortuguero and the surrounding communities.

Services

Pavona to Tortuguero

We are operating a daily service from La Pavona (Pococi) to Tortuguero via La Suerte River which is about a 60 minute ride from the final stop of the public bus.

We also offer customized services from other locations such as:

  • Caño Blanco
  • Puerto Viejo de Sarapiqui
  • Moin Limon
  • Barra del Colorado
  • Isla Calero
  • And more!
